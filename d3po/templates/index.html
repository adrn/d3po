D3: project one

an experiment in interactive plotting


What is all this?

D3PO is a project designed to allow an astronomer, with no specialized data visualization skills, to make an interactive, publication-quality figure that has staged builds and linked brushing across plots. Right now we are in search of alpha testers, who have figures that could be made interactive and who are willing to get their hands a little dirty (but no javascript skills needed). Full 1.0 version expected in Spring 2014.
